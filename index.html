USD - United States Dollar

The US Dollar (USD) is the most traded currency globally and a primary reserve asset.

EUR - Euro

The Euro (EUR) is the official currency of the Eurozone and ranks second in trade.

GBP - British Pound

The British Pound (GBP) is the world’s oldest currency still in circulation.

INR - Indian Rupee

The Indian Rupee (INR) is a vital currency for South Asia’s growing economy.

JPY - Japanese Yen

The Japanese Yen (JPY) is Asia’s most traded forex currency.

AUD - Australian Dollar

The Australian Dollar (AUD) is highly influenced by commodity exports.

CAD - Canadian Dollar

The Canadian Dollar (CAD) is widely respected for its economic stability.

CHF - Swiss Franc

The Swiss Franc (CHF) is known as a “safe-haven” currency.

CNY - Chinese Yuan

The Chinese Yuan (CNY) reflects China’s growing influence on global trade.
